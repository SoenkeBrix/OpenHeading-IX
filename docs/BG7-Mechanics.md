# BG7 Mechanics

Project: OpenHeading-IX  
Status: Concept

## 1. Function

BG7 covers mechanical mounting, orientation, enclosure and magnetic cleanliness.

## 2. Board coordinate convention

Preliminary convention:

- +X points forward
- +Y points starboard
- +Z points upward

This convention must later be checked against sensor package axes.

## 3. Magnetic cleanliness rules

- Keep magnetometer away from high currents.
- Avoid ferromagnetic screws near the sensor.
- Avoid DC/DC converters near the magnetometer.
- Keep NMEA2000 and power wiring away from the sensor board where possible.
