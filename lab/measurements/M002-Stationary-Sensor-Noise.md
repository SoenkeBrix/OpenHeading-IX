# M002 Stationary Sensor Noise

Project: OpenHeading-IX  
Status: Planned

## Objective

Measure stationary noise and offset of accelerometer, gyroscope and magnetometer.

## Procedure

1. Place the sensor platform on a stable non-magnetic surface.
2. Avoid touching the table during acquisition.
3. Record at least 1000 samples per sensor channel.
4. Calculate mean, standard deviation, minimum and maximum.

## Data fields

- ax, ay, az
- gx, gy, gz
- mx, my, mz
- temperature if available
