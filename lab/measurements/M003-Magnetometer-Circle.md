# M003 Magnetometer Circle

Project: OpenHeading-IX  
Status: Planned

## Objective

Record magnetometer X/Y/Z data during a slow 360 degree rotation.

## Purpose

This measurement is the basis for observing:

- hard-iron offset
- soft-iron distortion
- local magnetic disturbances

## Procedure

1. Place sensor on a non-magnetic rotating support if available.
2. Rotate slowly through at least one full turn.
3. Record mx, my, mz and timestamp.
4. Plot mx versus my.

## Expected observation

A perfect undisturbed sensor would form a centered circle in the horizontal plane. Real data will usually form an offset ellipse.
